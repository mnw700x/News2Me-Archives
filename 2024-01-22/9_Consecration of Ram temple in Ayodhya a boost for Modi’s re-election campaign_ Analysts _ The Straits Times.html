Consecration of Ram temple in Ayodhya a boost for Modis re-election campaign: Analysts

Nirmala Ganapathy
India Bureau Chief
Updated
1 Hour Ago
More

NEW DELHI The consecration of the temple for Lord Ram in Ayodhya will bolster Prime Minister Narendra Modis image as a strong nationalist leader who fulfils the aspirations of Indias majority Hindus, giving his 2024 re-election campaign a boost, said political analysts.

Mr Modi is already seen as the front runner in the upcoming general election, due before May, as he seeks a third consecutive term in power.
