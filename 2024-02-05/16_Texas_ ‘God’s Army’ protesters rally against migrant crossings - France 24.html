Texas: ‘God’s Army’ protesters rally against migrant crossings

Issued on: 05/02/2024 - 18:10Modified: 05/02/2024 - 18:44

Video by: Sam BALL

A convoy of trucks, vans and RVs from across America converged in southern Texas on Saturday to protest what they say is an "invasion" of migrants coming across the border with Mexico. Mixing religious rhetoric with vocal support for former president Donald Trump, the protesters had been dubbed “God’s Army” by one of the event’s organisers.
